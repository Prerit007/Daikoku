Our Mission

Our main focus is to bring aspiring people closer to innovation, by providing them with the tools to learn and a platform to connect with growing startups, with revolutionary ingenious ideas and the driving force of making a change. With the leading-edge technology, our ethos is focused on building a stronger ladder for the onset of their professional career.

Core Values

Innovation

The first pillar of our vision is innovation. We encourage startups to put up innovative projects while also giving students the freedom to decide the project's intricacies.


Transparency

We are helping students connect directly with the companies and interact with them without any "secret" screening process.


Accessibility

We wish to help students who cant find relevant projects to work on for their resume with the added benefit of working for corporates.
