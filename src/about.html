About the Project

Finding the Inspiration in Every Turn

This platform is a bridge between companies who require quick software solutions and students who want suitable projects to work on. Companies and startups provide a wide range of internship and small projects opportunities which the students can take up depending upon their field of interests and capabilities. Few projects are also provided with LOR or even PPO opportunities as per the rules of the company and the work done by the students. We are on a mission to equip students with practical exposure and knowledge to help them get a chance towards a better career by providing them a world full of possibilities.
