About the Project

Finding the Inspiration in Every Turn

The most common difficulty faced by students nowadays is finding a suitable project and the issue faced by various startups and companies is finding quick software solutions. We plan to tackle this situation by designing a platform to help connect both parties.

Our platform is well-structured and designed keeping in mind the following ideas of prime importance:

  • Companies and startups offer projects and request software solutions
  • Students can take up projects and complete them
  • Projects evaluated by SMEs and Top-3 projects are given LOR and Internship / PPO opportunities
  • All students who complete projects can be provided with the Certificate of Completion (Digital Certificate)
  • Feature to update profiles like any other competitive coding profiles.

Few functionalities the project provides:

  • Authentication (Google and Github)
  • Profile Creation
  • Project Filtering and Recommendation
  • Platform Currency
  • Recommended Courses
  • Chat Bot
  • Code Editor
  • Blogs Section for SEO
  • Digital Certificate Verification
